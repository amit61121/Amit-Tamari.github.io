Tableau project - Airbnb washington

In this project, I researched trends from Airbnb listings in Washington, revealing that prices tend to increase during holidays and summer vacation periods. Most guests show a strong preference for 1-bedroom rooms, selecting them three times more frequently than other options. These insights underscore the seasonal pricing dynamics and popular room choices that characterize Washington's Airbnb market.
